Troubleshooting:
1. Turn off your browser's Ad-blocker or tracking shield for this page.
2. Make sure you aren't opening this file straight from your desktop (use a local web server like Live Server).
3. Facebook videos: You must be logged into Facebook in another tab. The video also must be public and have embedding enabled by the page owner. Many Facebook pages disable embeds.
4. If a YouTube stream shows "unavailable," the video may be region-locked or private.
5. M3U8 streams need HLS.js support (loaded automatically).
